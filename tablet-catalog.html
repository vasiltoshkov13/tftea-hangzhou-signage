🌹 Our Heritage · 品牌传承

TFTea is born from the heart of Bulgaria's Rose Valley — the same valley that has produced the world's finest Damask roses for over 400 years. Our 400-acre bio plantation in Bunovo village has been growing roses since 1908.

TFTea诞生于保加利亚玫瑰谷的中心——这片山谷400年来一直出产世界上最优质的大马士革玫瑰。我们在Bunovo村的400英亩有机种植园自1908年开始种植玫瑰。

🌿 Our Promise · 我们的承诺

Every TFTea blend combines premium Chinese teas with authentic Bulgarian botanicals — hand-picked Damask rose buds, wild chamomile from the Balkan foothills, and lavender from our own fields. No artificial flavors. No shortcuts.
